About Us

Founded in 2015, Kelowna Collies seeks to find forever homes for misplaced, neglected or abused border collies.

Our non-profit organization recieves Collies, Australian Shephers, and Aussie Cattle Dogs (Blue/Red Heelers) from all over the United States and Canada and places them with capable and loving owners in the Okanagan Valley.
